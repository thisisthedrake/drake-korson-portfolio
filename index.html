Selected Works.

Antrum Booth

Experience Design

Antrum needed a big presence to showcase their centralized sensing tech—and it had to be modular enough to reconfigure for future shows. I took the concept design, detailed out how it would be built and assembled, and created a full 3D model. From there, I built a clear set of install instructions so the team could break it down, ship it, and set it up again without needing me onsite.

About Me.

Hey, I'm Drake — a designer, craftsman, storyteller, and generally a maker of fun things. I've worked across graphic design, 3D environments, physical builds, and UX/UI — and I bring all of that into the way I design for the web, games, and real-world products.

I believe that great design should be both aesthetically pleasing and functionally effective. Whether I'm crafting a product identity, an exhibit space, or a digital experience, I approach each project with a focus on creating meaningful connections between people and the things they use.
