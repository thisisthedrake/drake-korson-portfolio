About Me

Designer with a passion for creating meaningful experiences

Hey, I’m Drake — a designer, builder, and problem-solver with a love for systems, stories, and making things that actually help people. I’ve worked across graphic design, 3D environments, physical builds, and UX/UI — and I bring all of that into the way I design for the web, games, and real-world products. My mission? Keep it human, keep it useful, and have some fun along the way.

I believe that great design should be both aesthetically pleasing and functionally effective. Whether I'm designing a website, application, or brand identity, I focus on creating solutions that connect with users and drive results.
